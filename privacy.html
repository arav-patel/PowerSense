Privacy Policy

Simple, honest, and short.

We do not collect personal information

PowerSense does not ask for your name, email, or any identifying information. You do not need to create an account to use this tool.

Your audit answers stay on your device

All answers you enter in the audit form are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. They never leave your device and are never sent to any server.

We use anonymous analytics

PowerSense uses Umami Analytics to count page visits and understand how people use the site. Umami does not use cookies and does not track personal information. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

Feedback is optional and handled securely

If you choose to submit feedback using the feedback button, your rating and optional message are sent to Formspree, a secure form processing service. No personal information is required. Formspree does not sell your data. You can read their privacy policy at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.

You can clear your data anytime

To remove your audit results from your browser, simply clear your browser history and site data. There is nothing stored anywhere else to delete.
